Project Site Plan

Site Name

Site Name: BYU-Pathway Training Resources

Reason: This site provides essential training materials and resources for BYU-Pathway missionaries to enhance their learning experience.

Optional Domain: byupathwaytraining.org

Site Purpose

The website will serve as a hub for BYU-Pathway missionaries, offering training materials, video tutorials, FAQs, and downloadable resources to improve their effectiveness in assisting students.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find video tutorials for training new missionaries?
  • How do I submit a student referral form correctly?

Color Scheme

Primary Color: #0056b3 (Used for headings, buttons, and accents)

Secondary Color: #f4f4f4 (Used for background and section separation)

Typography

Heading Font: 'Montserrat', sans-serif

Body Font: 'Open Sans', sans-serif
